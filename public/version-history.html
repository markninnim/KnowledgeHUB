KnowledgeHUB™ Version History

A running record of major releases and improvements

v1.2 Lab & AI-Assisted Document Checks 17 July 2026

Introduced the Lab section, giving users AI-assisted first-pass checks on customer-submitted documents.

  • Added Payslip Check — forensic-style AI review flagging inconsistencies or signs of tampering.
  • Added Bank Statement Check, including an optional cross-check against an uploaded payslip to confirm stated income matches deposits.
  • Added a full "Checks Performed" breakdown and a Save PDF button to Lab reports.
  • Lab added to Feature Rollout Controls so it can be enabled per business/user group.
  • Added a footer credit line to the Home page.
v1.1 Broker Profile, Engagement & Reporting Enhancements 16 July 2026

A broad round of improvements to the Supervisor Zone, broker profiles, CPD tracking, and Home page.

  • Added a PDF export for the full Broker Profile report, plus Test Results, Consumer Duty/Understanding scoring, and Replacement Ratio to AutoCRM™ Renewals.
  • Added CPD Progress pace markers, specialist CPD targets, and licence icon indicators.
  • Added YTD vs prior-year Pay comparison charts and Feefo leaderboard fixes (rank pins, NPS).
  • Wired ReEngage™ to LeadGEN with round-robin assignment and referral tagging; moved AutoCRM™/ReEngage™ notes to Airtable so they persist across deploys.
  • Added Bespoke Social Content pillars and a Today's Recommended Post card to Home.
  • Numerous layout, spacing and consistency fixes across broker profile cards.
v1.0 Foundation 15 July 2026

Core platform structure and navigation put in place.

  • Reorganised top navigation (Supervise, Learn, Earn) and grouped AutoCRM™/ReEngage™/Engage tabs.
  • Moved feature flags and quick-links persistence from local files to Airtable.
  • Added licence icon badges and tooltips to broker cards on Supervise.
  • Added an AutoCRM™ Renewals summary to the broker profile view.
v0.1 Early Development Pre-July 2026

Initial build of the platform (then FPG Brand Hub) — password-gated brand asset repository with logo, template and guideline downloads, ahead of the wider feature build-out captured from v1.0 onward.
