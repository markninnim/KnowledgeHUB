KnowledgeHUB™ Version History

A running record of major releases and improvements

v1.2 Lab & AI-Assisted Document Checks 17 July 2026

Introduced the Lab section, giving users AI-assisted first-pass checks on customer-submitted documents.

  • Added Payslip Check — forensic-style AI review flagging inconsistencies or signs of tampering.
  • Added Bank Statement Check, including an optional cross-check against an uploaded payslip to confirm stated income matches deposits.
  • Added a full "Checks Performed" breakdown and a Save PDF button to Lab reports.
  • Lab added to Feature Rollout Controls so it can be enabled per business/user group.
  • Added a footer credit line to the Home page.
v1.1 Broker Profile, Engagement & Reporting Enhancements 16 July 2026

A broad round of improvements to the Supervisor Zone, broker profiles, CPD tracking, and Home page.

  • Added a PDF export for the full Broker Profile report, plus Test Results, Consumer Duty/Understanding scoring, and Replacement Ratio to AutoCRM™ Renewals.
  • Added CPD Progress pace markers, specialist CPD targets, and licence icon indicators.
  • Added YTD vs prior-year Pay comparison charts and Feefo leaderboard fixes (rank pins, NPS).
  • Wired ReEngage™ to LeadGEN with round-robin assignment and referral tagging; moved AutoCRM™/ReEngage™ notes to Airtable so they persist across deploys.
  • Added Bespoke Social Content pillars and a Today's Recommended Post card to Home.
  • Numerous layout, spacing and consistency fixes across broker profile cards.
v1.0 Foundation 15 July 2026

Core platform structure and navigation put in place.

  • Reorganised top navigation (Supervise, Learn, Earn) and grouped AutoCRM™/ReEngage™/Engage tabs.
  • Moved feature flags and quick-links persistence from local files to Airtable.
  • Added licence icon badges and tooltips to broker cards on Supervise.
  • Added an AutoCRM™ Renewals summary to the broker profile view.
v0.4 Leads, CRM & Opportunities Expansion 9–13 July 2026

Built out Muttuo/LeadGEN lead management, AutoCRM™ renewals, per-business branding, and a first version of the AI-assisted Help widget (later renamed KnowledgeHELPER™).

  • Added Muttuo and LeadGEN lead management tabs with pipeline stats, filters and a Data/MI dashboard.
  • Added AutoCRM™ tab showing mortgage renewals due in the next 6 months, scoped to each broker's own cases.
  • Added per-business theming (nav logo, brand navy/orange) driven from Airtable.
  • Added Whereabouts tab for supervisors/admins.
  • Introduced the first rule-based Help widget, later expanded with a ring-fenced AI layer and renamed KnowledgeHELPER™.
  • Added Wealth, Buy to Let, Commercial and Bridging opportunity pages with licenced-adviser referral sections.
v0.2–v0.3 Learning, CPD & Team Management 21 June–6 July 2026

Grew the platform from a brand asset repository into a full learning and team management system, with CPD logging, Knowledge Tests, and the Supervisor Zone.

  • Added the Learning section: video library, Knowledge Tests, Industry Reading, and Monthly Newsletters with CPD logging.
  • Added AutoCPD™ logging and CPD Progress tracking by Mortgage/Protection/Investment category.
  • Added My Team (Supervisor Zone) with broker cards, CPD progress bars and drill-down broker profiles.
  • Added Feefo review integration, leaderboards and company-wide ranking.
  • Added user management (admin): roles, product/licence pills, CSV import/export.
v0.1 Brand Consistency & Compliance Reporting 7–8 July 2026

Renamed the platform to KnowledgeHUB™, standardised styling sitewide, and added the compliance reporting forms.

  • Renamed the site to KnowledgeHUB™ throughout and introduced AutoCPD™ branding.
  • Added compliance reporting forms: Complaint, Breach, Conflict of Interest, Gifts & Hospitality, later joined by Self Sale and Whistleblowing.
  • Standardised page titles, subtitles, colours and button styles into a single style guide.
  • Added service tenure display and birthday indicators to broker profiles.
v0.0 FPG Brand Hub Origins 19–20 June 2026

The very first version of the platform: a password-gated brand asset repository, before it grew into KnowledgeHUB™.

  • Initial build: password-gated site with downloadable logos, templates and guidelines.
  • Added social asset cards (LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram) with preview and download.
  • Added brochures section and the original Staff Buys area, later renamed LeadGEN.
  • Redesigned the layout to the topbar-plus-sidebar structure still in use today.
