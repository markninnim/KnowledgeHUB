# KnowledgeHUB™ Demo Checklist

## PART 1 — Broker view

### Home
- Personalised greeting — "Good morning/afternoon/evening, [First Name]!" based on time of day; automatically swaps to "🎂 Happy Birthday, [First Name]!" on the broker's own birthday
- Customer Birthdays widget — pulls today's client birthdays automatically, auto-rotates through name + email if there's more than one
- Quick Links tile grid — drag-and-drop reordering, click the edit icon on any tile to change its destination
- AutoCRM™ mini-widget — renewals-by-month strip + running total, right on Home
- News Bulletins scroller (supervisors/admins can click "Add Story" to post one); Latest Learning Video card and Latest Newsletter card sit alongside it
- FP Surveying / Acre Surveying partner card
- CPD Progress snapshot (bars by Mortgage/Protection/Investment) and ReEngage™ snapshot, side by side
- Consumer Duty card — Consumer Duty Score %, three clickable stat tiles (Full Understanding / Understanding Restored / Partial Understanding) that filter a "Recent Responses" list below
- Feefo card (full width):
  - Star rating summary + paginated list of the broker's own reviews
  - Company-wide rank badge (e.g. "#1 of 42") next to the "Your Feefo Reviews" heading
  - Date-range filter on reviews (From/To + Clear)
  - "Top Reviews" and "Top NPS" mini-leaderboards alongside the review list

### Compliance — AutoCPD™
- Log CPD entries (activity, date, minutes, category, Mortgage/Protection/Investment type, what you learned)
- Specialist CPD breakdown for licensed subjects (Lifetime Mortgages, Commercial, Bridging, PMI, Business Protection, Wealth) — shows % of parent CPD target
- CPD Progress bars with YTD pace tick mark ("Ahead"/"Behind" of where you should be)
- Period filter: Month / Quarter / Year
- CPD pie chart (Mortgage vs Protection split)

### Compliance — Reporting forms (six shared forms)
- Complaint
- Breach
- Conflict of Interest
- Gifts & Hospitality
- Self Sale
- Whistleblowing

### Compliance — Reading library
- MAS (Mortgage Advice Standards) — full standards document, sections + annexes/appendices
- PAS (Protection Advice Standards) — full standards document, sections + appendices (incl. PMI, Business Protection, Whole of Life, etc.)

### Learning
- View Live — join scheduled Zoom sessions, auto-logs 50/50 split CPD
- Weekly / Induction / Revalidation training recordings
- Monthly Newsletters (logs 20 min CPD on read)
- Industry Reading articles (timed CPD)
- Knowledge Tests — scored, pulls from the Revalidation Score base (4 tests, pass mark shown)
- Fitness & Properness questionnaire
- PowerPoint download button on video player

### Marketing / Brand Hub
- Graphics library
- Stationery templates
- Brand Colours reference
- Typography reference
- Brand Voice guidelines
- Broker Branded Logo personaliser (download your own branded logo)
- Business card, moving card, DIP certificate, CPD record PDF generators

### Earn / Opportunities ("Selling Zone")
- Cross-sell prompts by category: Remortgage, Lifetime Mortgage, FTB Mortgage, Commercial Mortgages, Bridging Finance, Wealth, PMI, Trusts, Surveying, Removals, Reviews & Referrals
- Each specialist card lists Licenced Advisers to refer to (with contact details + Feefo reviews)
- Conversation-starter scripts and "why it matters" guidance per opportunity

### Surveying
- FP Surveying vs Acre Surveying partner comparison, referral links/commission info

### Pay
- Monthly commission/pay statements (PDF, by month)
- YTD earnings comparison chart — this year vs last year, % change badge
- Loading spinner on slow data pulls

### Engage™ (LeadGEN)
- Lead list with statuses, Golden Hour / Today / Follow Up filters
- CSV import/export

### ReEngage™
- Re-engagement/lapsed-client workflow, notes modal

### AutoCRM™ (Mortgage Renewals)
- Renewal pipeline by month (6 → 1 month out sidebar)
- Business Won toggle per client
- Notes modal per renewal
- Replacement Ratio stat (new business vs lost business, YTD, with info tooltip)

### Whereabouts
- Team location/diary visibility (if enabled)

### My Account
- Contact details, job title, licences held, salutation
- Headshot/photo (used on Opportunities referral cards)

### Help widget (site-wide)
- Ask questions about site features, "who do I refer to," "how many brokers do we have," etc.
- Strict rule: never shares one broker's client/lead data with another

## PART 2 — FPG Supervisor view (everything above, plus:)

### My Team (Supervisor Zone)
- Adviser cards/list with CPD progress bars (Mortgage/Protection/Investment), colour-coded status (Complete/On track/Behind)
- Specialist licence icons — turn green once 3 hours YTD CPD logged in that subject, tooltip shows progress
- 🎂 birthday indicator next to adviser name on their birthday
- Non-CAS flag + tenure length for non-CAS advisers
- Click into a broker card → full Broker Profile drill-down (see below)
- CSV export of team CPD
- Transfer adviser between supervisors

### Broker Profile (supervisor drill-down)
- Header: specialist licence icons held
- CPD Progress + Specialist CPD breakdown for that broker
- Test Results — 4 Knowledge Test scores (from Revalidation Score base), pass/fail
- Consumer Understanding card — logged CPD "% full", turns green at 100%
- Consumer Duty Score — turns green at 100%
- Feefo reviews summary + company-wide rank ("#N of Y advisers by review count") next to NPS
- Videos Watched (capped list, "+N more" expandable) and Revalidation videos (same pattern)
- Newsletter Access card
- Engage™ card (leads) — auto-hides if broker has no Engage data, other cards reflow to fill the row
- ReEngage™ card — auto-hides if no data
- AutoCRM™ Renewals card — Replacement Ratio (YTD, fixed calendar-year, ignores page date filter) with explanatory tooltip
- Pay card — monthly commission list + YTD comparison chart (this year vs last year)
- PDF export of the full Broker Profile report
- Loading spinner while profile/pay data loads

### Compliance Reporting (admin/supervisor)
- View all six Reporting form submissions across the team (Complaint, Breach, Conflict of Interest, Gifts & Hospitality, Self Sale, Whistleblowing)

### User Management (admin)
- Users table — product/licence pills, CSV import/export
- Nav-tab access toggles per user (which tabs a broker sees)
- LeadGEN / Marketing / Supervisor / Admin role flags

### Numbers (Performance Zone)
- Data/MI dashboard — stats, PDF export

### Muttuo
- Data dashboard — call/lead stats, PDF export

### News Bulletins (Add Story)
- Post a new bulletin shown on all brokers' Home tab
